Kebab

Freshly grilled kebab with thin‑sliced tender meat, crisp veggies and house sauces. Stuffed in pita or wrap, it’s a hearty classic you won’t get tired of. Pick your favorite sauce and add cheese for extra comfort.

Kebab Spit

Kebab meat, slowly cooked on the rotating spit, is the secret to its authentic and rich flavor. Stacked carefully, it turns gently, allowing the flavors to develop while keeping the meat juicy and tender. This traditional cooking method ensures a melt-in-your-mouth texture with a slightly crispy crust on the outside, delivering a delicious bite every time.

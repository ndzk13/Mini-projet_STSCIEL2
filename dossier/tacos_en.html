Tacos

Street‑food style loaded tacos: marinated meats, golden fries, melty cheese and a creamy signature sauce. Pressed on the griddle for a crunchy shell and a gooey center.

Tacos Composition

A tacos brings together a choice of meat such as chicken, beef, kebab or even nuggets, paired with crispy fries tucked inside. It can be topped with fresh veggies like lettuce, tomatoes, onions or peppers, and enriched with melting cheeses such as cheddar, mozzarella or raclette. Everything is finished with a generous sauce, whether it’s Algerian, barbecue, samurai or a creamy cheese sauce.
